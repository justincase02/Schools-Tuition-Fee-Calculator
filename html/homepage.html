About us

The UB Learning Resource Center supports the mission vision of the university by providing academic resources vital to liberal education.

Teaching research skills and promotes information literacy necessary for lifelong learning. It aims to serve as a hub of information and gives quality services to the whole university community.

Vision

The UB Learning Resource Center will be an innovative, inspiring, and integral partner in the scholarly achievements of UB students, faculty, employees and other members of the UB Community. In so doing, we actively contribute to the development of knowledge and the advancement of society.

View details »

Mission

The Learning Resource Center supports the mission of the University by providing academic resource vital to liberal education, developing research skills and promoting information management literacy necessary for lifelong learning. The UB LRC actively supports the discovery, creation, and preservation of knowledge. Develop collections and services that support and encourage the academic endeavors of students, faculty, staff, and administrators, build a community of critical consumers of information.
